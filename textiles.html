Textile Arts

I'm happiest when I've got multiple crafting projects going on, especially when I am learning a new technique simultaneously. After many years learning quilting patterns, I turned my hands to sewing handbags from textile industry waste. Limiting myself to using scraps inspired my creativity and built upon my graphic design and quilting experience. I sold my bags at craft shows in 2017 and 2018.
