Darcy & Taylor

Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop

For an Illustrator class, I recreated a brogue shoe in a realistic style. Taking the project further, the brogue became the launching point for a shoe retailer and product line: Savile Row Collection. The fictitious brand is an international retailer of fine clothing with a focus on menswear. Located in the Savile Row district of London since 1915, it has launched its own polish brand to reinforce a new collection of bespoke leather shoes, notable for saturated color options in traditional styles.

The target audience is men age 40+ with an annual base income $100,000/ £77,000. The advertising challenge that is met in this campaign is to encourage a new generation of men to care for their luxury shoes, and to give traditional shoe care a modern makeover.

Brogue Illustration and Branding



Poster Campaign

The challenge: Not many people keep leather care or shoe kits anymore. Keeping one's shoes looking sharp needs to be as enticing as the shoes. The solution: I created posters to remind the target audience that daily life has opportunities to distinguish oneself.



Web Ads

Using references from entertainment culture (James Bond and Firefly fandoms), these web ads utilize humor to connect with the target audience. The two-fold “Call to Action" is Care for your shoes with our polish; distinguish yourself with our shoes.
